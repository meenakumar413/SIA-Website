Introduction to AI & ML

Welcome! This presentation introduces the fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

What is Artificial Intelligence?

  • The simulation of human intelligence in machines
  • Applications include problem-solving, speech recognition, and decision-making
  • Can be narrow (specific tasks) or general (broad tasks)

AI enables machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.

What is Machine Learning?

  • A subset of AI that enables systems to learn from data
  • Algorithms improve performance without being explicitly programmed
  • Includes supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning

Machine Learning allows AI systems to adapt and improve over time based on experience.

Types of Machine Learning

  • Supervised Learning: labeled data training
  • Unsupervised Learning: finding patterns in unlabeled data
  • Reinforcement Learning: learning via rewards and penalties

Understanding these types helps you choose the right algorithm for a task.

Popular AI & ML Tools

  • Python libraries: TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn
  • Data visualization: Matplotlib, Seaborn
  • Cloud AI services: Azure, AWS, Google Cloud

These tools make building and deploying AI solutions more accessible.

Real-World Applications

  • Healthcare: diagnosis and predictive analytics
  • Finance: fraud detection, algorithmic trading
  • Retail: recommendation engines, inventory management

AI and ML are already transforming industries worldwide.

Ethical Considerations

  • Bias in training data
  • Transparency and explainability
  • Impact on jobs and society

Responsible AI practices are essential for fairness, safety, and trust.

Final Takeaways

  • Understand core AI & ML concepts
  • Learn about popular tools and frameworks
  • Explore ethical considerations and real-world applications

This foundation prepares you to explore deeper AI and ML topics with confidence.
