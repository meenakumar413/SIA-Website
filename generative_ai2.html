Introduction to Generative AI

Key concepts on Generative AI

Welcome!

This presentation introduces key concepts on Generative AI.

What Is Generative AI?

  • AI that creates new content
  • Examples: text, images, music, code
  • Powered by deep learning models

Generative AI goes beyond prediction—it generates original outputs based on learned patterns.

Real-World Applications

  • Chatbots and virtual assistants
  • Image and video generation
  • Code completion and debugging

From customer service to creative industries, generative AI is transforming how we work and interact.

Key Technologies

  • Neural networks and transformers
  • Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Training on massive datasets

These technologies enable machines to understand and replicate human-like creativity.

Popular Tools

  • ChatGPT, DALL·E, Midjourney
  • Hugging Face Transformers
  • Google’s Bard, Meta’s LLaMA

These tools make generative AI accessible to developers, educators, and creators.

Ethical Considerations

  • Bias in training data
  • Copyright and originality
  • Misinformation risks

With great power comes responsibility—ethical design and usage are essential.

Final Takeaways

  • Learn the fundamentals
  • Explore real-world tools
  • Practice with guided labs

This is just the beginning—continue learning and experimenting to unlock the full potential of generative AI.
