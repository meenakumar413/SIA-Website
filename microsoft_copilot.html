Streamlining Your Work with Microsoft Copilot

Welcome! This session explores how Microsoft Copilot can enhance productivity across daily workflows, from writing to data analysis.

What Is Microsoft Copilot?

  • Integrated into Microsoft 365 apps
  • Uses natural language to assist tasks
  • Available on web, desktop, and mobile

Copilot is embedded in tools like Word, Excel, and Outlook, helping users complete tasks faster using AI.

Core Chat Functions

  • Ask simple or complex questions
  • Get citations for fact-checking
  • Save responses to editable pages

Copilot Chat is versatile—great for brainstorming, research, and organizing ideas with traceable sources.

Prompt Engineering

  • Use clear, specific language
  • Try step-by-step instructions
  • Combine voice and text input

The better your prompt, the better the output. Copilot supports both typed and spoken prompts.

Content Creation Tools

  • Draft emails and documents
  • Summarize long content
  • Rewrite for tone and clarity

Copilot can help you write faster and more effectively—perfect for reports, emails, and proposals.

Visual & File Capabilities

  • Generate images from prompts
  • Upload files for analysis
  • Extract insights from documents

Copilot isn’t just for text—it can analyze files and generate visuals to support your work.

Copilot in Microsoft 365 Apps

  • Summarize emails and draft replies
  • Analyze data and create charts
  • Generate reports and documents

Copilot Pro integrates directly into your favorite Microsoft apps, making everyday tasks smarter and faster.

Privacy & Settings

  • Adjust privacy settings
  • Clear chat history
  • Understand data usage

You’re in control. Copilot offers transparency and tools to manage your data and privacy preferences.

Real-World Use Cases

  • Project planning and task lists
  • Meeting summaries and follow-ups
  • Data-driven decision-making

From managing meetings to analyzing spreadsheets, Copilot adapts to your workflow and saves time.

Final Takeaways

  • Embrace AI for everyday tasks
  • Practice prompt writing
  • Explore Copilot in your favorite apps

Copilot is a powerful tool—start small, experiment, and let it evolve your productivity.
