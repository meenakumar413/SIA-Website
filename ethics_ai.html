Ethics in AI -->

Skills and Innovation Academy (SIA)

Fairness and Bias

  • Ensure AI decisions are unbiased and equitable
  • Identify and mitigate dataset bias

AI should treat all users fairly, avoiding discrimination based on race, gender, or other factors.

Transparency

  • AI systems should be explainable
  • Users must understand how decisions are made

Transparency builds trust and accountability in AI systems.

Privacy

  • Protect personal data and comply with regulations
  • Minimize unnecessary data collection

AI systems must respect user privacy and store data securely.

Safety and Responsibility

  • AI should not cause harm
  • Developers must be accountable for AI behavior

Responsible AI design ensures technology benefits society safely.

Inclusive Innovation

  • Consider diverse perspectives in AI development
  • Encourage accessibility and equity

Ethical AI empowers everyone and supports social good.
