AI Tools Overview

Welcome! This section explores popular AI tools that enhance learning, productivity, and creativity.

We’ll cover ChatGPT, Git & GitHub, Notebook LM, Google Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, and Perplexity — showing how each supports innovation and collaboration.

ChatGPT

  • Conversational AI for writing, brainstorming, and coding help.
  • Understands natural language and generates responses instantly.
  • Used in education, customer support, and creative industries.

Speaker Notes: ChatGPT helps learners and professionals think, write, and problem-solve more effectively.

Git & GitHub

  • Version control system and collaborative coding platform.
  • Tracks project changes and allows multiple people to work together.
  • Essential for AI, web, and software projects.

Speaker Notes: Git helps manage versions of your work, while GitHub connects teams in real time.

Notebook LM

  • AI-powered research and note assistant by Google.
  • Helps organize information and generate summaries from documents.
  • Ideal for students, educators, and content creators.

Speaker Notes: Notebook LM acts like an intelligent research partner that helps you study or prepare lessons faster.

Google Gemini

  • Google’s next-gen AI model integrated across apps like Docs, Gmail, and YouTube.
  • Provides multimodal capabilities — text, images, and voice.
  • Focuses on reasoning, summarization, and creativity.

Speaker Notes: Gemini is designed to be a true productivity partner, blending with your daily tools.

Microsoft Copilot

  • Integrated into Microsoft 365 for real-time AI assistance.
  • Generates documents, presentations, and insights from your data.
  • Enhances workflow automation and decision-making.

Speaker Notes: Copilot helps professionals work smarter, using data-driven insights and automation.

Perplexity

  • AI search engine that combines accuracy with conversational answers.
  • Cites reliable sources for every response.
  • Great for research, exploration, and continuous learning.

Speaker Notes: Perplexity blends the best of search and AI — perfect for students and educators researching topics responsibly.

Final Takeaways

  • Each AI tool supports creativity, collaboration, and productivity.
  • Experiment with different tools to find your ideal workflow.
  • Stay curious and keep learning — the AI landscape evolves fast!

Speaker Notes: Using AI tools responsibly helps students and professionals thrive in a tech-driven future.
