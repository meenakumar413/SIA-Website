AI Tools Overview

Welcome! Explore some of the most popular AI tools transforming learning, productivity, and creativity.

ChatGPT

  • Conversational AI for questions, writing, and coding.
  • Supports brainstorming, learning, and content creation.
  • Easy-to-use interface for beginners and experts alike.

Think of ChatGPT as your personal AI assistant to help with text, code, or general knowledge queries.

Git & GitHub

  • Git: tracks changes in your code (version control).
  • GitHub: online platform for collaboration and sharing projects.
  • Essential for teamwork and code management.

Git is like a time machine for your projects; GitHub lets teams work together efficiently.

Notebook LM

  • Interactive AI-powered notebooks for coding, research, and learning.
  • Supports natural language queries inside notebooks.
  • Great for experimentation and teaching AI concepts.

Notebook LM allows users to combine AI assistance with live coding environments.

Google Gemini

  • Next-generation AI model by Google.
  • Capable of multimodal tasks: text, images, and more.
  • Ideal for advanced AI applications and creative projects.

Gemini enables developers and researchers to leverage state-of-the-art AI capabilities.

Microsoft Copilot

  • AI assistant integrated with Microsoft 365 applications.
  • Automates writing, summarizing, and data tasks in Word, Excel, and Teams.
  • Boosts productivity in the workplace.

Copilot helps users get more done with AI directly inside familiar Microsoft tools.

Perplexity

  • AI search engine combining chat and verified answers.
  • Answers queries with concise explanations and references.
  • Useful for research, fact-checking, and learning.

Perplexity brings AI-powered insights directly to your questions with reliable sources.

Final Takeaways

  • These AI tools enhance learning, creativity, and productivity.
  • Experiment with each tool to understand its strengths and applications.
  • Stay curious and explore the AI landscape responsibly.

Mastering these tools will give you a practical edge in AI-driven work and study.
